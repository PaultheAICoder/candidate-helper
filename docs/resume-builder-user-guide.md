# Resume Builder User Guide

## Overview

The Resume Builder helps you create a professional resume through a guided, 5-step process with AI-powered assistance. Whether you're starting from scratch or importing from LinkedIn, we'll help you build a standout resume.

## Getting Started

### Access the Resume Builder

1. Navigate to `/resume-builder` or click "Build Resume" in the navigation
2. You can use it as a guest (saved in browser) or sign in for cloud storage

### Guest vs. Registered Users

**Guest Users:**
- Resume saved in browser for 7 days
- All features available
- Cannot sync across devices
- See "Sign up" banner

**Registered Users:**
- Resume saved to cloud
- Access from any device
- Auto-migrate guest drafts on sign-in
- Integrate with interview practice

## The 5-Step Process

### Step 1: Basic Information

Enter your contact details:
- **Full Name** (required)
- **Email** (required)
- **Phone** (optional)
- **Location** (City, State)
- **LinkedIn URL** (optional)

**Pro Tip:** Click "Import from LinkedIn" to auto-fill fields by pasting your LinkedIn profile content.

### Step 2: Work History

Add your professional experience:

1. **For each job:**
   - Company name
   - Job title
   - Start/end dates (check "I currently work here" if applicable)
   - Brief description of responsibilities

2. **AI Enhancement:**
   - Describe your work in your own words
   - Click "Enhance with AI"
   - Get 2-3 professionally-written bullet points
   - Select suggestions or edit them

**Example:**
```
Your input: "Managed social media"

AI suggestions:
• "Managed social media accounts for 5+ clients, increasing engagement by 40%"
• "Led social media strategy across Instagram, Twitter, and LinkedIn"
• "Developed data-driven content strategy that grew followers from 2K to 15K"
```

**Important:** AI asks clarifying questions if your description lacks specifics. It NEVER fabricates numbers or metrics.

3. **Add the job** to your resume
4. **Repeat** for all positions (1-10 jobs supported)

### Step 3: Education & Skills

**Education:**
- Degree (required)
- Institution (required)
- Field of study (optional)
- Graduation year (optional)
- Add multiple degrees if needed

**Skills:**
- Type skills and press Enter or click "Add"
- Click X to remove skills
- Mix technical and soft skills

**Pro Tip:** The AI will suggest skills based on your work history.

### Step 4: Professional Summary

Generate a compelling summary:

1. Click "Generate Summary"
2. Review 3 AI-generated options
3. Select your favorite
4. Edit to personalize
5. Or click "I'll write my own summary"

### Step 5: Review & Export

**Preview:**
- See your complete resume
- Click "Edit" on any section to make changes
- Live preview updates as you edit

**Export Options:**
- **PDF** - Most compatible, best for printing
- **Word (DOCX)** - Editable in Microsoft Word
- **Text (TXT)** - Copy-paste into application forms

**Use for Practice:**
- Click "Use This Resume for Interview Practice"
- Your resume uploads automatically
- Redirects to tailored interview practice

## Features

### Auto-Save

Your resume saves automatically every 30 seconds. Look for "Saving draft..." indicator.

### LinkedIn Import

1. Go to your LinkedIn profile
2. Click "More" → "Save to PDF" or copy the page
3. Paste content into the import modal
4. Review extracted data before proceeding

**Note:** We don't access LinkedIn directly. You control what data to share.

### AI Content Enhancement

**What it does:**
- Transforms weak descriptions into strong achievements
- Follows STAR framework (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
- Uses action verbs and quantifiable metrics
- Asks questions when details are missing

**What it doesn't do:**
- Fabricate accomplishments
- Invent numbers or metrics
- Make false claims

### Multiple Export Formats

**PDF:**
- Professional formatting
- ATS-friendly (no tables)
- Standard fonts
- Proper spacing

**DOCX:**
- Editable in Word
- Standard headings
- Bulleted lists
- Preserves structure

**TXT:**
- Plain text format
- Easy copy-paste
- Works in any form
- No formatting

## Tips for Best Results

### Writing Work Descriptions

**Good examples:**
- "Increased sales by 25% through targeted outreach"
- "Led team of 5 developers on project delivery"
- "Reduced costs by $50K annually through process improvement"

**Could be improved:**
- "Responsible for sales"
- "Worked on development projects"
- "Helped reduce costs"

### Using AI Suggestions

1. Be specific in your initial description
2. Include numbers when possible (team size, budget, impact)
3. Mention tools/technologies used
4. If AI asks questions, update your description and re-enhance

### Skills Selection

**Include:**
- Technical skills (software, tools, languages)
- Soft skills (leadership, communication)
- Industry-specific knowledge
- Certifications

**Avoid:**
- Overly generic ("hard worker", "team player")
- Unverifiable claims
- Too many skills (15-20 is ideal)

## Troubleshooting

### Resume not saving

**Check:**
- Internet connection (for registered users)
- Browser localStorage not disabled (for guests)
- Try refreshing the page

### AI enhancement not working

**Try:**
- Make description longer (10+ characters minimum)
- Add more context about your role
- Check internet connection
- Refresh and try again

### Export download failed

**Check:**
- Pop-up blocker settings
- Browser download permissions
- Try different format
- Refresh and re-export

### LinkedIn import not working

**Try:**
- Copy more content (50+ characters)
- Include both work history and education
- Paste plain text (not HTML)
- Try manual entry instead

## Privacy & Data

### Guest Users
- Data stored in browser only
- Expires after 7 days
- No account required
- Not synced

### Registered Users
- Data stored securely in Supabase
- Only you can access your resume
- Auto-deleted if you delete account
- One-click data export available

### What We Don't Store
- LinkedIn passwords or credentials
- Social Security Numbers (automatically stripped)
- Date of Birth (automatically stripped)
- Audio or video recordings

### Common Questions
**Q: How long does it take?**
A: 15-20 minutes for most users

**Q: Can I edit after exporting?**
A: Yes! Just return to the builder and your draft is saved.

**Q: Can I use this resume for applications?**
A: Absolutely! Export as PDF for most applications, DOCX for ATS systems that prefer Word.

**Q: Does signing up cost anything?**
A: No, the resume builder is completely free.

**Q: Can I delete my data?**
A: Yes, registered users can delete from settings. Guest users can clear browser data.
